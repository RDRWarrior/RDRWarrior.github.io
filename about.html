Who I Am

I am a hybrid technical game designer and systems-focused developer with experience building gameplay systems, interactive tools, and collaborative creative projects. My work sits at the intersection of technical implementation, player experience, and structured iteration.

Technical Focus

My strongest areas are gameplay systems, iterative prototyping, interaction design, and technical structure. I work across Unity, C++, C#, and interactive software projects, with an emphasis on readable mechanics, practical implementation, and systems that can grow over time.

I am especially interested in projects where engineering decisions directly shape usability, player clarity, and overall design quality.
